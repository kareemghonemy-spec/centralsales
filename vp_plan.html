Support Required — Kedar's Team (Ads Division)
🤝 What we need from Kedar's team to execute this plan
6 workstreams — each mapped to a bucket impact
🔍
Pre-Campaign Assessment
Before brands commit NCR budgets, they want to know the expected ROI. We need Kedar's team to provide a pre-campaign model: projected impressions, estimated incremental orders, and CPiO benchmarks for the brand's category — delivered as a 1-pager to KAMs before pitching.
Bucket: New/Unlock + Retain
📊
How to Sell the Results
KAMs need a post-campaign playbook — a standardized way to present results back to brand partners. What metrics matter, how to frame CPiO vs growth, and how to use performance data to renew or upsell commitments. Kedar's team to build a reusable results deck template.
Bucket: Maintain + Upsell
🔄
Retention / Upsell Workstream
A structured quarterly workstream between KAM and Ads to identify: (1) brands at risk of churning budget, (2) brands ready for upsell, and (3) brands to push into new ad tools. Monthly syncs with data shared 2 weeks before QBRs so KAMs can walk in prepared.
Bucket: Retain + Upsell
🔑
Keyword & CTR Search Query Reports
For brands questioning their ad ROI (especially Epik), we need keyword-level and search query reports — which queries triggered their ads, CTR by keyword, and impression share vs competitors. This gives tangible proof of visibility even when conversion is soft.
Bucket: Retain (Epik focus)
🎯
Ad Strategy — New Customer Acquisition
Brands like Nando's and Subway need to see ads driving net new customers, not just re-ordering regulars. Kedar's team to provide a "new customer" targeting strategy per brand — audience segments, optimal ad formats, and estimated new customer CPO by category.
Bucket: New/Unlock
📡
Traffic Data + Better Reporting
KAMs currently lack traffic attribution data — how many page views, search appearances, and click-throughs brands receive from their ad spend. We need a shared reporting dashboard (weekly refresh) with GMV, orders, CTR, impressions, and CPiO per brand — visible to both KAM and the brand.
Bucket: All buckets
